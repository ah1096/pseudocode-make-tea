Steps to make tea:
  Get a mug
  Choose tea: black, white, green
    Open jar
    Put tea in infuser
    Put infuser into mug
Boil water according to tea chosen
    Put water in kettle
    Push button for correct temperature
    Put boiled water into mug
Add honey, lemon, milk, sugarcube
    Put additive into tea
    Stir
END/ enjoy tea
